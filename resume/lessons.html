Lessons Learned


The most important lesson we've learned as a team is just how important communication is for a project - something we've struggled with in the beginning stages. Regardless of how innovative or useful a product may be, if the concept is not communicated clearly, it will fall flat. Learning to simplify explanations and concisely outlining the details of our application is what ultimately improved our communication skills.

We've also learned that when our work is being evaluated not to take constructive criticism personal - it's for the betterment of the overall design. Keeping this in mind, we've successfully increased the ease of use and efficiency of the interaction flow within our application.
