Summary of Results

Interviews

  • 9 total responses. Majors ranged from Engineering and Information Technology to Nursing and Psychology. 44% Seniors 44% Juniors and the rest Sophomores.
  • Regardless of if the students felt awkward asking random students for help or not, when tough work needs to be done, they typically search for classmates to work with - emphasizing the need to find others.
  • The vast majority of the students mentioned they tend to have more productive study sessions with strangers.
  • Students like to use materials such as post its, whiteboards, and text messaging to supplement their study sessions
  • The bottom line is that students want to get their classwork done well and know that finding like minded individuals can aid them in achieving that goal.

Surveys

  • We had 39 total responses. Average age of participant is 21 with most being Engineering majors and the rest a very diverse background ranging from National Security and Music Technology and Philosophy, surprisingly our number of ITI students was very low.
  • Roughly 82% of students prefer to study after 6PM, with the average number of sessions being 2-5 times a week.
  • 59% of students say their study sessions are not planned.
  • Most of the students who say they typically study in their dorm rooms also indicate that they study alone - but also indicated they would like to interact with other students.
  • 94% students prefer to study in person rather than over the internet.
  • A majority of students prefer to communicate with each other through texting and other instant messaging platforms.
  • More students would rather go to fellow classmates for help instead of emailing their professors.
  • Most students are open to the idea of studying with strangers.
