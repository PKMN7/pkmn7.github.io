Data Collection

During the semseter we sent out a Google Form and collected data relevant to students' studying behaviors and their thoughts on study groups. Below we have catalogued the questions we used during surveys and interviews. We have also provided some insight into students' and their academic life through the responses we received.

Interviewer Script

Hello my name is ______ and I am a (programmer, designer, etc) for an application aimed at improving the way students study and conditions in which they study here at Rutgers. Would you mind if I asked you a few questions about yourself and how you study, especially in groups?

Interview Questions

  1. Name, Major, Year?
  2. Do you ever find it weird or awkward to ask random students in class for help? Why?
  3. Is it ever difficult to find other people to study with exactly when you need to? If yes, why?
  4. What are some factors that influence your decision to study alone or with other people?
  5. When working in groups, do you tend to get more work done with strangers or with friends?
  6. What platforms do you like to use to communicate with study partners?(Slack, GroupMe, etc.) and what do you like about these particular platforms?
  7. What things do you like to use to study with?(Post its, flashcards, whiteboards, etc.)?
  8. Is there anything you wish that was a bit easier when it comes to studying or finding people to study with?

Survey Questions

  1. What time do you usually study?
  2. How often do you study?
  3. How long are your study sessions?
  4. Are your study sessions planned or more sporadic?
  5. Where do you typically study?
  6. Do you prefer to study alone, or with a group?
  7. Are you open to studying with people you might have not known prior to a study session?
  8. Do you typically like helping others study?
  9. When studying in a group, do you prefer studying with the group in person or through digital means?
  10. When communicating with study partners, what method do you use most?
