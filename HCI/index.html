Introduction

Problem Statement

If there is one common theme amongst college students nationwide it is this, studying matters. A great deal of us are developing new study habits on the go and with the demands of our daily class and work schedules those sessions are sporadic throughout the week (59% of students interviewed). Students want to work with others to study and learn in person (94% of those surveyed) but their resources to find students to study for the same subjects are limited and it is difficult to interact with groups of strangers to get work done. This leaves students with limited networks isolated in their troubles; the ability to find like minded individuals in the same courses will aid a student in achieving their goals.

Our Solution

Here with StudySesh, we've developed a functional applciation designed to help students form or attend study groups anywhere at anytime for class in which they are registered. Our applcation lets students know of study groups happening around them and displays location and group information across course sections and instructors.

Major Tasks

  1. Find reliable study groups using StudySesh application.
  2. Solidify study groups going forward and build on campus network.
  3. Allow students to study efficiently at any time within their hectic schedules.
