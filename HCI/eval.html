Project Description for Heuristic Evaluation

Name

Study Sesh

Team

Study Sesh - Studyseshteam@gmail.com

Goal

To help students easily find classmates to study with.

Purpose

Provide an easy way for students to both know the locations of all on-going study sessions that relate to classes they're taking, and easily create study sessions to attract others.

User

The target users are current college students who have a need to do classwork with others.

Tasks

  • Look at what study sessions are going on and some information about them
  • Create study sessions for other classmates to attend
  • Add/remove classes you are currently taking

Limitation

  • There is no way to “join” study sessions in the app; you join the session by simply showing up to the location that you see on the map.
  • No automatic session size count; creator of session manually increments or decrements number
  • Currently optimized for iPhone 6 without support for other systems.
