Our Game


Infection.exe


Get ready to explore the inner networks of the working world in Infection.exe!

    Infection.exe is a game of domination and control inwhich players take control of nodes on a network. The game helps teach players programming concepts like:

  • If / Else: Player decisions and other aspects affect gameplay
  • Variables: Variables are taught through the form of a counter mechanism that determines the games runtime.
  • Data Structures: Players learn to efficiently traverse the ever changing network of nodes.
  • Modularity: Players can design networks that they want to play on, and we feel this helps better the learning process as each game played with a different approach
